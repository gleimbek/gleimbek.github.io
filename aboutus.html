About Penny Juice

Since its inception in 1983, Penny Juice has remained unwavering in its commitment to crafting premium, all-natural juices. For over two decades, we've strived to create beverages that are not only delicious but also packed with wholesome goodness. Our dedication to using only the freshest and healthiest ingredients, combined with our strict adherence to FDA standards, has set us apart as a trusted name in the industry. As we continue to grow, Penny Juice is embarking on an exciting journey to expand our presence in the market, both domestically and internationally. Our core principles of product excellence and affordability remain at the heart of our mission. We believe that everyone should have access to high-quality, natural juices that not only taste great but also contribute to a healthier lifestyle.

Penny Juice - Leadership Team

President and CEO

Thomas Reid joined Penny Juice in June 2017 bringing more than 15 years of experience working in the agricultural industry, most recently as CEO of Penny Juice. He began his career in public accounting and guided business clients, primarily in the agricultural industry in financial optimization.

Chief Financial Officer

Melody Howmarth joined Penny Juice in April 2021 bringing more than 15 years of experience working in manufacturing and food processing. She began his career at Price Water House Coopers advising clients across many industries about concentrating on processing.

President Operations

Robert Sanz joined Penny Juice in 2007, as the Director of Operations responsible for manufacturing, production, and safety. After, he was promoted to VP and acquired management responsibilities for Quality and Food Safety, Engineering, and Supply Chain Management.

President of Marketing

Brad Roger joined Penny Juice on April 10, 2022 and is a strategic CPG marketing executive with over 10 years of experience leading iconic brands such as Bush's Baked Beans, Boar's Head, Pillsbury, Rubbermaid and Summit Hill Foods, representing brands recognized domestic.

Human Resources

Sally Welk joined Penny Juice in 2004 and has served as Vice President since 2009. She has over 10 years of human resource and change leadership experience in multiple industry sectors including manufacturing, health care, business process outsourcing, and social service non-profit.

Penny Juice is an American company that produces concentrated juices of the highest quality at competitive prices. Representing the best quality in its products with high nutritional content for anyone who wants to enjoy a good snack free of additives and artificial sweeteners.
