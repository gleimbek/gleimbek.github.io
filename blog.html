Healthy fruits

Today's lifestyle causes more and more people to suffer from stomach pain. If this is your case, we recommend that you take care of your diet and avoid foods that are too acidic or contain a large amount of starch. Fruits and vegetables are especially recommended, since they provide a large amount of fiber, starch and pectin.

Banana that surprises

The banana is not only one of the most consumed fruits in the entire world, but also one of the healthiest. Due to its caloric and vitamin content, it is indicated for children, pregnant women, nursing mothers, athletes and the elderly. Its flavor is sweet and delicious, it is a fruit rich in vitamins C and B6, and essential minerals.
